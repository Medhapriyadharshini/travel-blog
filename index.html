I'm Santhosh George Kulangara

I'm an Indian Traveller,Television producer,Director,Broadcaster,Editor and Publisher.Also the founder of Safari TV, a channel dedicated to travel and history-based programmes.As of 2021, i had travelled to more than 130 Countries and the journeys are telecated through Sancharam,the first travel documentary in Malayalam.Now l'm the Kerala State Planning Board Member of Tourism

I was the first person in India to go in Space as Tourist. I had my first training of zero gravity training at the Kennedy Space Centre, Florida…Me and 12 other trainees flew in aircraft designed to give the zero gravity experience.

"And if a common man can fly to space, I should be the first person from India to go to space because I have taken travel as a profession"

- Santhosh George Kulangara

Keralaism

An avid traveller who has explored more than 130 countries across seven continents. As he compared those countries to his home state, Kerala, several thought-provoking questions arose in his mind. This book seeks to find the possibilities of answering those questions. It is a work of immense relevance in the present day scenario of Kerala.

Sancharam

Santhosh George Kulangara, a famous Malayalee who is a world traveller, has prepared a rare book for those who want to travel abroad. Singapore, Malaysia, Sri Lanka, Thailand, Dubai, Bali, China, Nepal, Hong Kong and Bhutan are the top 10 countries that can travel with the required amount to travel around India.

Zero Gravity

Zero Gravity This book details the wonderful experience the author had while undergoing zero gravity training on board a specially equipped aircraft over the Atlantic Ocean as part of the preparations for his journey to space. It lucidly portrays the unique feel one has while being in a gravity free atmosphere.
